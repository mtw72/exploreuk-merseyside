  Sefton  |  Liverpool  | Wirral

Discover the charming Southport waterfront, one of the many scenic attractions in Sefton. Enjoy the golden beaches, take a leisurely stroll along the second-longest pier in the UK, and admire the panoramic views of the Irish Sea. At the Atkinson Arts Centre, you can explore interesting exhibitions, attend exciting performances, and participate in engaging workshops.

Sefton

Located in Liverpool, the Sefton area harmoniously fuses urban vibrancy with captivating natural beauty. One of its standout gems is Crosby Beach, featuring the renowned art installation “Another Place” by Antony Gormley. From there, venture into Sefton’s vibrant city center, where cultural experiences, historical landmarks, and picturesque landscapes await. Immerse yourself in the tapestry of this enchanting destination, attracting both visitors and residents alike.

Liverpool

Liverpool City Centre pulses with vibrant energy, seamlessly blending a rich historical tapestry with contemporary allure. Stroll along its iconic waterfront, where architectural marvels such as the renowned Liver Building grace the skyline. Lose yourself in the lively streets teeming with eclectic shops, lively pubs, and the intoxicating ambiance of a thriving arts scene. Venture deeper into the heart of the city to uncover its fascinating spirit, woven into the fabric of its diverse neighborhoods and cultural landmarks.

Wirral

Embark on a wonderful journey through the Wirral, situated just across the water from Liverpool City Centre.

Discover the intriguing history of Birkenhead Priory, admire the elegance of Hamilton Square's neoclassical buildings, and revel in the natural beauty of Wallasey Beach. With its breathtaking landscapes, rich heritage, and warm hospitality, the Wirral invites you to explore and experience the wonders of this enchanting region.

Wallasey Beach

Wallasey Beach entices with its vast stretch of sandy shores, gentle waves, and panoramic views of the Irish Sea. This calm coastal destination invites visitors to relax, unwind, and immerse themselves in the tranquil beauty of this glamorous beach.

Hamilton Square

Hamilton Square, a remarkable architectural gem, showcases one of the finest Georgian squares in the UK. With its well-preserved gardens, neoclassical buildings, and elegant charm, it unveils a window into the area's rich history and timeless allure.

Birkenhead Priory

Birkenhead Priory, a revered historical site, stands as a testament to the town's past. Its serene grounds and ancient ruins provide a stunning glimpse into the region's heritage. From here, visitors can enjoy a panoramic view of Liverpool, providing a peaceful retreat from the bustling modern world.
